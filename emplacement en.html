Brainstorming is a problem-solving technique under the guidance of a moderator, brainstorming specifically an informal meeting to gather ideas or puzzles for children. The term brainstorming also refers to a puzzle game that appeared in the late 2000s on the Internet. The general idea of this method is a collection of many original ideas. Two principles define brainstorming: the suspension of judgment and the search for varied ideas.These two principles translate into four rules: do not criticize, let go, to return to what is shown, to seek to have the greatest number without imposing its ideas.Therefore, absurd and unusual proposals are allowed in the phase of production and mutual stimulation.

Regardless of the method, most brainstorming techniques have 3 steps: First, write down your ideas, discuss your ideas and evaluate them, and then determine which ideas will be put into practice. There are several methods of brainstorming The “How Now Wow” matrix, Mind maps, Gap analysis etc… In the case of my group of friends and myself we use the “How Now Wow” matrix this technique is to rank ideas in How , Now or Wow in terms of their degree of innovation and ease of implementation. “How” for good ideas but not concrete , “Now” for ideas can original but easy to implement and to finish the most important and what we are looking for the most “Wow” innovative ideas and that we can implement right now.
