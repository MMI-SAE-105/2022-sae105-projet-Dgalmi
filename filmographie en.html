Elon Musk is an engineer, inventor, investor, and visionary business leader who has changed the way we think about transportation, space travel, renewable energy, and much more. He is one of the richest entrepreneurs in the world. He was named one of the 100 most influential people by the magazine TIME in 2007 and 2016.

How did Elon Musk become a billionaire?

Elon Musk became a billionaire by creating many company that have been successful. He didn’t start out as a billionaire. Musk created his first company when he was about twenty, and it was called Zip2. The company was acquired by Compaq in 1999 and Musk earned $22 million on the sale of Zip2. Musk’s second company was X.com , which was an online financial services and e-mail payment company. It was bought back by what became PayPal in 2002, and Musk received $180 million from the sale . When his friend and co-founder of Tesla, Martin Eberhard, was looking for investors for Tesla, Musk invested $10 million in the company. He also became the CEO of Tesla after that. Musk has made several other successful investments and acquisitions through Tesla, including the $2 billion purchase of SolarCity.
