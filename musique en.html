Xto1 - Music

Zkr, named Zacharya, was born in Roubaix in October 1996 to Tunisian and Moroccan parents. A good student until the end of high school, he dropped out of high school and left for second grade.Incarcerated from November 2018 to May 2019, he changed his life when he got out of prison to devote himself to rap.He made a name for himself by rapping 5-minute freestyle songs that he published on YouTube.Hard and direct lyrics and his street style in these freestyle songs made him famous. In November 2018, a first EP entitled Absence est sorti, the title referring to his time in prison.He then released his first album “Dans les mains”, which is released on January 8, 2021 and ranks first in sales in France the week of its release. Its album that evokes the passage behind bars was certified gold in June 2021 to February 2022, it releases Chameleon on March 4, 2022.

The “working zone” music refers to his life before rap so to his life before he starts to make a good living and legally he makes us understand that this working-class and harmful zone “Where many are lost, where even their hope hanged” and “It’s hard, I come from a working-class zone” to make us also understand that even when working hard financially it’s not the amount of work In this working-class area there is also a lot of criminal activity, her thirst for money pushing her to do something “It is true that it is illegal but it is maxi-profitable at the time, we allowed ourselves to do it”

The instrumental was composed by Skillano, Julien Poinsotte & DJ Bellek and producte by AllPoints France, Morning Glory Music & All Road Prod
